About Me

Hello! I’m Yoav, a graduate student at Mor Geva's lab specializing in NLP with a focus on interpretability in LLMs. I'm currently working on research in knowledge adaptation for LLMs, and SAE feature interpretability.

Projects

  • Large Scale Knowledge Adaptation in LLMs (ongoing): Researching novel methods of implanting a large amount of new knowledge into existing LLMs, ensuring that the new knowledge is used by the model.
  • SAE Feature Interpretation (ongoing): Working on a new method to automatically create interpretations of SAE features in LLM.
  • Parkalot: Created an app to help find free spots in busy parking lots.
